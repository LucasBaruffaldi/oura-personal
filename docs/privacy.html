Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

Lucas Oura Export is a private, personal-use application operated solely by its owner. It is not offered as a service to other users.

Data accessed

With the account owner's explicit authorization, the application may access Oura data covered by the permissions selected during OAuth authorization, including sleep, readiness, activity, heart rate, workout, session, tag, blood oxygen, stress, heart health, personal-profile, and ring-configuration data.

Processing and storage

The application downloads authorized data directly from Oura to the owner's local computer. Credentials, tokens, and exported health data are stored locally and are not intentionally transmitted to an application server, sold, rented, used for advertising, or shared with other users.

Oura processes data according to its own privacy policy and terms. This application is independent from and is not endorsed by Oura Health Oy.

Retention and deletion

The owner controls retention. Local exports and OAuth tokens can be deleted at any time. API access can also be revoked through the owner's Oura account.

Security

Credentials, tokens, and exports are excluded from the source-code repository by default. The owner is responsible for securing the computer and the local files where data is stored.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the public source-code repository associated with this application.
